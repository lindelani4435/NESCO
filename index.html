Why Choose Us

Trusted non profit donation center

Our Mission: To strengthen community development for improved populations livelihoods' improvement through training, mentorship, marketing and comprehensive operational facilitation in Africa. To promote work and saving culture at community level through self-help group economic activities.
Our Objectives: Empower self-help groups in Africa through training, guidance counselling and market diversification. To promote economic activities among women and the youth through self-help groups. To create awareness on reduction of environmental degradation mitigation.
Our Company Values: To strengthen community organisations networking for advanced operations through education, skills and technology exchange and transfer. To promote talents, search and nurturing with special focus on youth women and PWDs through collaboration with national governments
